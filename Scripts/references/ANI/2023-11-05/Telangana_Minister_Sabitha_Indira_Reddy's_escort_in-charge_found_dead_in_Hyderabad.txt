<p>Hyderabad (Telangana) [India], November 5 (ANI): The <a href="/topic/police-escort">police escort</a> in-charge of Telangana Education Minister Sabitha Indira Reddy was found dead in Hyderabad's Jubilee Hills area, the Hyderabad City Police informed on Sunday.<br/>The deceased has been identified as 60-year-old, Mohammed Fazal Ali.<br/>According to the West Zone DCP of Hyderabad City Police, D Joel Davis, the police suspect suicide owing to the financial and family issues of the deceased.<br/></p><p>"The victim who allegedly died by suicide is the escort in-charge of Minister Sabitha Indira Reddy. The victim is identified as Mohammad Fazal Ali of 60 years' age. The incident happened at around 7 a.m. We suspect that the reason for this might be financial and family issues. The forensic team is on the spot," DCP Davis said.<br/>DCP further informed that the police have started an investigation into the matter.<br/>"The postmortem examination (PME) is yet to be done. Case is yet to be registered, the complaint is being filed," DCP Davis added.<br/>Further details on the incident are awaited. (ANI)<br/></p>

KOCHI: Two Assamese migrant labourers, who have been working in a sawmill in Muvattupuzha, were found dead in their rented room at Adooparambu with cuts on their necks on Sunday.The deceased have been identified as Mhantho and Dipankar Basuma. Police suspect it to be a case of murder and have launched a search for a fellow migrant worker who stayed with the two.A local resident from the area told reporters that when he went to the workers' room, he found them lying down covered with a sheet."It appeared as if they were sleeping. Then I saw the pool of blood near their beds. Thereafter, I informed the police. The incident must have happened in the morning," he said.

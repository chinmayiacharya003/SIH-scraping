NAVI MUMBAI: Heavy and light vehicles will not be allowed to use the carriageway towards Mumbai on Mumbai-Pune Expressway on Thursday (today) for 6 hours - from 11am to 5pm. As Mumbai Railway Vikas Corporation Ltd will carry out work on Panvel-Karjat suburban rail corridor, the authorities have diverted traffic at Panvel exit to travel along old Mumbai-Pune highway (NH48) via Karanjade and reach Kalamboli circle and then move towards Mumbai, said assistant police inspector Yogesh Bhosale.Vehicles on NH48, from Pune towards Mumbai, will be diverted from Shedung junction bypassing Borle toll plaza to reach Panvel.
